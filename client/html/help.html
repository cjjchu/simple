FAQ

1、Main purpose of this APP

To apply or not apply an DB2 for z/OS PTF to production system is a question. System administrators often consider many factors before making a Software maintenance decision, and they are very cautious when implementing the changes. The IBM z Service Advisor mobile App is a smart z Systems software maintenance solution with a mobile App client. This app assists administrators of z Systems software to easily access z Service information from IBM. It pushes the urgent service information such as HIPERs, PEs or Red Alerts for DB2 and CICS to the mobile client, and it provides the impact evaluation and recommendations to customers to facilitate the maintenance decision. It also can help customers check and understand the maintenance status/risk of the system.

2、Searching

To quickly locate PTF with a particular PTF number or keyword, click the Search icon on the main page to display the Search view.

3、Customize

If you only want to view or get the notification of PTFs you care about, click the filter icon on the main page to display Preference view.

By selecting the keywords your are interested in, you can customize what PTFs will be pushed and presented to you.

4、WishList

After reviewing the information of a PTF, if you wish to come back and review again or take action later, touch the "bookmark" icon at the top right corner of PTF summary/detail view, it will be added to your WishList.

5、Feedback

In the Account view, use Feedback function to tell us what you are thinking about our App or any suggestions to us.
